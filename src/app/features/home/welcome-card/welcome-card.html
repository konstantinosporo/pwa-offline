PWA Offline Mode Presenting offline-first experience with angular/pwa.

This application:

  1. Works offline
  2. Queues actions while offline
  3. Syncs actions when back online

Note: Online deletion and update are disabled for testing. Offline deletion is still functional and will be queued until you're back online.
